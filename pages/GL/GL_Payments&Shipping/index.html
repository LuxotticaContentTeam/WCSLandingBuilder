Buying with insurance

Paying with insurance offers flexibility to buy prescription eyeglasses or sunglasses. Once logged in, your in-network benefits automatically apply in cart—no additional paperwork needed. If you are out-of-network, we have reimbursement forms you can submit to your carrier.

Paying with Insurance

You can use your FSA or HSA card to pay directly for all your purchases on Glasses.com. Or checkout using your preferred payment method and then submit your receipt directly to your FSA or HSA provider for reimbursement.
